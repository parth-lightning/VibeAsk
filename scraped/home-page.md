# Government Polytechnic College, Barmer

Government Polytechnic College Barmer, an organization under the Department of Technical Education, Government of Rajasthan. It is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated with the Board of Technical Education Rajasthan. It was established in 1988-89 by the central government under Border Area Development program.
