About St. Mary's College

St. Mary's College is a premier institution of higher learning, committed to providing quality education and fostering holistic development of students. With over 125 years of excellence, we have produced graduates who have made significant contributions to society.

Our beautiful 50-acre campus provides a serene environment for learning, with state-of-the-art facilities, modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and an extensive library.
